Spaghetti Aglio Olio for Busy Professionals

A typical pasta dish typical of the city of Naples, Italy. This recipe will include a simplified twist made for people in a hurry.

If you're looking for something healthy, quick, tasty without spending too much time in the kitchen, this recipe is for you.

Ingredients per serving

  • Pasta, 450g
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil, 125ml
  • Thinly sliced garlic, 3 coves
  • Chili flakes, 1.25g
  • Salt, 0.5g
  • Pepper, 0.5g
  • Finely chopped Italian Parsley, 60g
  • Parmesan Cheese, 250g

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pit of water and lightly salt the water with 10g of salt.
  2. Once it's boiling, drop 450g of pasta into the boiling water. Let it simmer away for 10-12 minutes. Check on its texture to ensure its el dante and not to overcook it.
  3. Heat a frying pan and pour doused it with some olive oil just enough to cover the entire surface. Start Sauteing the finely chopped garlic once the oil is hot.
  4. Strained pasta from the pot and transfer the pasta into the pan. Add the chili flakes, salt and pepper into the pan. Start mixing until evenly flavored.
  5. Plate the pasta. Add the finely chopped Italian parsely, extra virgin olive oil and parmesan cheese over the pasta.
  6. Mix it around and your meal is ready.
